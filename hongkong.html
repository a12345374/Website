About Hong Kong

Edutrip Info Top Universities

#1. City Life

Hong Kong is a fast-paced, modern city with a blend of skyscrapers, shopping, and nature. The public transport system is efficient, and there are plenty of parks and hiking trails for a quick escape from the city buzz.

#2. Food & Drinks

Famous for its food, Hong Kong offers everything from dim sum and street snacks to high-end international dining. Locals and tourists enjoy milk tea, roast meats, wonton noodles, and street snacks like fish balls and egg waffles.

#3. Culture

Hong Kong’s culture blends Chinese traditions with Western influences. Festivals, temples, and family values mix with modern art, fashion, and global trends, creating a unique mix of old and new.

#4. Weather

  • Spring (Mar–May): Warm and humid, light rain. 18°C to 28°C.
  • Summer (Jun–Aug): Hot, rainy, 30°C average.
  • Autumn (Sep–Nov): Sunny, dry, 20°C to 28°C.
  • Winter (Dec–Feb): Cool and dry, 12°C to 20°C.

#5. Education

Hong Kong is known for its high-quality education system, strong academic performance, and internationally ranked universities. It attracts many international students through exchange programs and English-taught degrees.
