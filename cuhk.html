Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK)

A global university rooted in tradition and innovation

University Overview

Location: Sha Tin, Hong Kong

QS Global Ranking: #36

Top Majors

  • Medicine and Public Health
  • Business Administration and Economics
  • Engineering and Information Technology
  • Social Sciences and Psychology

Student Composition: Local – 60%, International – 40%

Programs & Opportunities

CUHK offers more than 280 exchange partnerships worldwide, dual degree programs, state-of-the-art research institutes, and interdisciplinary training aimed at nurturing global leaders with a deep commitment to community and innovation.

Fun Facts

  • CUHK has the largest campus in Hong Kong, spanning over 137 hectares of greenery.
  • It is the only university in Hong Kong with a collegiate system.
  • CUHK pioneered the first Chinese-language MBA program in the world.
  • The university emphasizes bilingual education (English and Chinese) in teaching and research.
