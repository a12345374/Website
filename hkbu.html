Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU)

Innovative learning with a strong liberal arts foundation

University Overview

Location: Kowloon Tong, Hong Kong

QS Global Ranking: #36

Top Majors

  • Communication and Journalism
  • Film, Media, and Visual Arts
  • Business and Marketing
  • Chinese Medicine

Student Population: Approx. 11,600 students

Programs & Opportunities

HKBU provides exchange programs with over 300 partner institutions, internship opportunities across Asia and beyond, service-learning courses, and interdisciplinary research programs that prepare students to lead and innovate in diverse global fields.

Fun Facts

  • HKBU was established in 1956 as a Christian liberal arts university.
  • The Academy of Film is one of the top film schools in Asia.
  • HKBU is home to the largest Chinese Medicine Teaching and Research division in Hong Kong.
  • The school emphasizes Whole Person Education, combining academic learning with ethical and civic development.
